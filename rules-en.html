Welcome to the rules page of Tic Tac Toe game modes! Here, you will find detailed explanations of the different variants of the game, so that you are not lost during your games. Take the time to read each rule carefully to understand the specificities of each game mode. Have fun and good luck!

Rules of Classic Tic Tac Toe

The classic Tic Tac Toe is played on a 3x3 grid. (9 cases in total). Two players face off, one using "X" and the other using "O". The goal for the players is to align three of their symbols in one of the following directions: horizontally[1], vertically[2] or diagonally[3]. The player who succeeds in aligning three of their symbols first wins the game. If all the squares on the grid are filled without any player managing to align three identical symbols, the game ends in a draw.[4]
Players take turns playing, placing their symbol in an empty square on the grid. A draw will determine who starts the game. Simple, isn't it?
