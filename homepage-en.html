The famous Tic-Tac-Toe game playable on the web!

What if I told you that playing Tic-Tac-Toe on paper is a thing of the past? With this website, I can assure you that playing old-fashioned Tic-Tac-Toe will be a thing of the past. All you need is a screen (PC or phone screen), a quiet place, and an internet connection. After that, gone are all those times when you had to get out your paper and pen to play this game, find a spot on the already scribbled-on Tic-Tac-Toe grid, and then search for another blank sheet to scribble on even more… poor trees that had to endure all those pencil strokes! 😔

Different game modes of Tic-Tac-Toe

Yes, you read that right, there will be different game modes available for playing Tic-Tac-Toe, but before we dive into the details, all the game modes (and I emphasize ALL)) share the same basic rule: two players must place shapes (X and O) in a grid of 9 squares, and the first player to line up three identical shapes wins the game, regardless of which way you read it. Simple and effective. From a single database, several modes can be created: a classic version, a game mode against an AI, perhaps a 5x5 mode, and many others that could spring from the mind of a web developer 👀. With all that, if you say you're bored, I don't know what else to do…
