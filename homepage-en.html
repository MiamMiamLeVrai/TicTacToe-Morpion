The famous Tic-Tac-Toe game playable on the web!

What if I told you that playing Tic-Tac-Toe on paper is a thing of the past? With this website, I can assure you that you won't be changing anytime soon 👀. All you need are a few things: a screen, a quiet place, a simple internet connection, and that's it. No more of those moments when you had to get out your paper, something to write on, and then, once the game was over (which took a minute tops), find a spot on the already scribbled-on sheet of paper with the Tic-Tac-Toe grids, search for another blank sheet to scribble on, and start all over again endlessly… poor trees that had to endure all those pencil or pen strokes 😔.

Different game modes of Tic-Tac-Toe

Of course, there will be different game modes available for playing Tic-Tac-Toe, but before we dive into the details, ALL game modes share the same rule: a maximum of two players must place (X and O) shapes in a grid of 9 squares, no more. The first player to align three identical shapes wins the game: a simple game with simple rules… KABOOM!!! 🤯. Furthermore, this database allows for several modes: a classic version (which is none other than Player vs. Player mode), a game mode against an AI, or a 5x5 mode 👀, etc. In short, plenty of ideas that could spring from a web developer's brain. With all that, if you say you're bored, I don't know what else to do…
