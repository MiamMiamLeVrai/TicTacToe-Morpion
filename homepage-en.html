The famous Tic Tac Toe game playable on the web!

What if I told you that playing Tic Tac Toe on paper is over now? With this website, I can assure you that playing the classic version of Tic Tac Toe will be quickly forgotten. You only need a screen and an internet connection, and after that, all those boring hours and waiting for a simple appointment are over.

Different game modes of Tic Tac Toe

We all know how to play Tic Tac Toe (if you don't know how to play, this is the right place to discover it) : two players must place shapes (crosses and circles) in a 9-square grid, and the one who aligns identical shapes wins the game. Simple and effective… but who says I haven't implemented new features to spice up your games ?
Between the classic mode, a countdown mode, against the AI, or a 2-player (available soon), if you say you're bored, I've at least tried my best…

It's time to start a game!

Enough talking, it's time to get serious and launch into the heart of the matter! Just below, you'll find a button that will start a Tic Tac Toe game, but before that, you'll need to choose the available game modes. That same button is also present in the navigation bar, so no worries. And all of this directly in your browser, no need to install anything unknown to anyone.
